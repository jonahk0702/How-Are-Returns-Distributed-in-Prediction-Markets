RETURNS & PROFITABILITY

- realized_return_ratio() – Realized profit relative to cost.
- mark_to_market_return(exchange) – Realized + unrealized return.
- get_total_return(exchange) – Net return over all trades.
- calc_simple_cash_multiple(portfolio) – Cash out vs. cash in.
- calc_cost_basis_returns(portfolio) – Return based on average cost.
- return_on_invested_capital(exchange) – (Realized + unrealized) / total capital.
- per_trade_profitability() – Avg % profit per closed trade.
- calculate_time_weighted_return(...) – Compounded return over trades.
- calculate_win_rate(...) – % of profitable closed trades.
- calculate_profit_factor(...) – Gains / losses.
- calculate_trade_expectancy() – Avg profit adjusted for win/loss rate.
- calculate_kelly_criterion() – Optimal % of capital to bet.


RISK & VOLATILITY

- calculate_volatility_of_return() – Std dev of returns.
- calculate_sharpe_ratio(...) – Risk-adjusted return.
- calculate_risk_of_ruin(...) – Probability of going bust.
- calculate_max_drawdown() – Worst peak-to-trough drop.
- calculate_largest_exposure(...) – Largest capital at risk.
- calculate_stop_loss_index(...) – How consistently stop-losses are used.
- calculate_position_sizing_score(...) – Aggressiveness of trade sizing.
- calculate_return_consistency(...) – Lag-1 autocorrelation of returns.
- calculate_return_skewness(...) – Skew of return distribution.
- calculate_return_kurtosis(...) – Fat-tailedness of returns.
- calculate_return_mad(...) – Robust return dispersion.
- calculate_outlier_ratio(...) – Frequency of return outliers.


BEHAVIORAL / STYLE-BASED

- calculate_momentum_score(exchange) – Trend-following tendency.
- calculate_mean_reversion_score(exchange) – Contrarian trading tendency.
- calculate_risk_tolerance() – Preference for low- or high-priced assets.
- calculate_order_clustering_score(...) – Bursty / rapid-fire trading.
- calculate_intraday_closeout_rate(...) – Same-day trade closure frequency.
- calculate_trade_sequencing_score(...) – Tendency to increase size after loss.
- calculate_trading_confidence(...) – Correlation between trade size and profit.
- calculate_diversification_index(...) – Spread across markets.
- calculate_gini_bet_size() – Inequality of trade sizes.
- calculate_gini_return_distribution() – Inequality of trade outcomes.
- calculate_news_reaction_metrics(...) – Metrics for reacting to news.
- calculate_news_alpha(...) – Trader’s edge over market on news.
- generate_news_reaction_table(...) – Summary of all traders’ news reactions.


TIME & HOLDING-BASED

- calculate_average_holding_time() – Days between buy and sell.
- calculate_trade_duration() – Avg duration positions held.
- calculate_relative_exit_efficiency(...) – Did trader sell at good moment?
- calculate_panic_sell_score(...) – Detects emotion-based exits.


MARKET-SPECIFIC / RESOLUTION-BASED

- outcome_adjusted_return(correct_outcomes) – Adjusted return if outcomes are binary.
- calculate_information_gain_from_price(...) – Did trader infer the market outcome?


INTERNAL TOOLS / HELPERS

- _get_closed_trades() – Constructs clean matched trades.
- infer_starting_balance(exchange) – Estimates starting cash.
- calculate_log_return_from_orders(exchange) – Log return from cash in/out flow.
- calculate_market_reaction_stats(people, news_events) – Avg market reaction.
- calculate_average_portfolio_risked(exchange) – Risk per BUY trade.
